DU/ER

UK

NEW FOR FALL/WINTER 2019, OUR MOST INNOVATIVE DENIM TO DATE. Featuring all the amazing stretch, comfort and durability of our Performance Denim, our Weatherproof Denim is lined with a water and windproof membrane, allowing for maximum protection from the elements.!

Elegant, trendy and taste-of-the-art, this down jacket is made for you !"

FR

HERE’S WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW It looks like premium denim for maximum versatility but is lined with a water/windproof membrane for weather protection, along with pops of reflective detailing for added visibility while commuting. This jean maintains a high level of a stretch (sitting at 21% where our original L2X Performance Denim is 30%) and features a signature DUER gusset. Don’t let the waterproof membrane scare you, both the jean and the membrane are 100% breathable allowing excess body heat to exit the garment. We decided to not seal the seams as we know the importance of a breathable garment. You can go from outside to indoors, without feeling like you need to change.

LET’S GET SCIENTIFIC The soft-shell membrane has been tested and received a water-resistant rating of 18,150mm (industry standard for water-resistance is 10,000mm). The MVTR (moisture vapour transmission rating) is 13,000 (compared to the industry standard for moisture wicking fabrics of 10,000 G/ M2/M24hrs). HERE’S WHAT YOU GET Denim 70% Cotton 28% Polyester (COOLMAXTM) 2% Lycra Waterproof Membrane 100% Polyester Reflective Detailing Found on the inside cuffs, seams and leather patch.

GALANCK

Young French start-up founded in 2016, Galanck was thought and created by Léa GALICE and Fabien PLANCKE. This company is engaged in active mobility and micro-mobility in urban areas, with the strong conviction that concrete solutions are necessary for their development. The solution aims to improve the movement of the entire community of soft mobility users (bicycles, segway, scooters, skate ...).

SAXX

UK

How Our Story Began Originality often times means being first and SAXX’s heritage of revolutionizing underwear goes back 10 years. In 2006, Trent Kitsch was on a fishing expedition and after spending hours in a cold, clammy ocean suit he knew there had to be a better way to protect his package from chafing. A former baseball player, he imagined a catcher’s mitt hammock design and turned it upside down in his mind. After the fishing trip, and with the hammock idea still percolating, he worked with a seamstress and designer — and himself as the fit model — to create a paneled fabric hammock that separated his balls from his legs. Fourteen prototypes later, the BallPark Pouch™ and SAXX Underwear were ready for action. Today, our focus remains the same: to equip you with game-changing, next-level apparel that allows you to move freely, without restriction — apparel that feels like it’s barely there, that elevates your everyday experiences, that just works.
